The Artist

About Tabs

Bio

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Edge

Tabs Vacilio is a Houston-based tattoo artist rooted in Japanese traditional tattooing with a contemporary twist. Drawing from Irezumi heritage — hannya masks, chrysanthemums, waves, and folklore — every piece carries the weight of centuries-old tradition executed with modern precision.

Primarily working in black and gray and geometric blackwork, Tabs builds compositions that are precise, bold, and deeply intentional. Clean linework, controlled shading, and a mastery of negative space define the work — whether it's a large-scale geometric piece, a black and gray portrait, or a dotwork study.

Equally at home in sacred geometry, ornamental patterns, and detailed black and gray realism, Tabs brings versatility without compromising craft. Her illustrative range spans everything from bold character work to fine-detail shading — always executed with the same disciplined foundation.

If you're looking for an artist who works across styles with the same level of precision and care — you found her.

If you're looking for an artist who respects the tradition and isn't afraid to evolve it — you found her.
