The Artist

About Tabs

Bio

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Edge

Tabs Vacilio is a Houston-based tattoo artist rooted in Japanese traditional tattooing with a contemporary twist. Drawing from Irezumi heritage — hannya masks, chrysanthemums, waves, and folklore — every piece carries the weight of centuries-old tradition executed with modern precision.

Her signature style bridges Japanese traditional work with geometric blackwork and dotwork patterning. Bold, confident linework meets intricate stippled shading and sacred geometry — compositions that honor the craft's roots while pushing it forward. Whether it's a full Hannya mask backpiece or a kawaii-inspired character study, every tattoo is built on the same foundation: disciplined technique and obsessive attention to detail.

Beyond the traditional work, Tabs brings a playful anime and kawaii influence into select pieces — proof that range and depth aren't mutually exclusive. From large-scale Japanese sleeves to intimate geometric compositions, every piece carries the same standard: sharp execution, intentional design, zero shortcuts.

If you're looking for an artist who respects the tradition and isn't afraid to evolve it — you found her.
