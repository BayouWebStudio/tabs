About Tabs

Bio

Where Fandom Meets
Fine Craftsmanship

Tabs Vacilio doesn't just tattoo anime — she translates obsession into permanent art. Based in Houston, TX, Tabs has carved out a lane where manga panels become skin-deep masterpieces. Every piece starts with the source material: the exact weight of a Naruto brushstroke, the melancholic linework of Nana, the kinetic energy of a Jujutsu Kaisen fight sequence.

Her signature style fuses bold, confident linework with stippled shading and geometric patterning — compositions that feel like they were ripped from a graphic novel and pressed into skin. It's illustrative tattooing with an editorial edge. Clean, intentional, and unmistakably hers.

But it's not just about the big custom pieces. Tabs keeps the culture accessible with regular flash deals — curated drops that sell out fast and bring her cult following back again and again. From palm-sized icons to full sleeves, every piece carries the same standard: obsessive detail, sharp execution, zero shortcuts.

If you've been waiting for an artist who actually understands the work you love — who knows the difference between a filler episode and a canon arc — you found her.
