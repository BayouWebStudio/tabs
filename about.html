About Tabs

Bio

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Edge

Tabs Vacilio is a Houston-based tattoo artist specializing in bold geometric and blackwork designs. Working from a private studio, Tabs focuses on precision, flow, and custom compositions that complement the natural structure of the body.With a strong emphasis on symmetry, pattern work, and high-contrast black and grey, each piece is thoughtfully designed to create a timeless and visually striking result. From full sleeves to large-scale body work, every project is approached with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.Appointments are available by request only. Clients can submit inquiries through the booking form or contact directly via email to begin the consultation process.
